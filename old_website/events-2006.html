EVENTS 2006



Quality Forum conducts many programs, lectures, seminars and events, sometimes in association with other leading organizations.

For future programs, please look at the EVENTS page.

For more information about some of the events conducted in the past, please visit the EVENTS page, and then select the year.


DECEMBER 18, 2006


We had an interactive session of members on December 18, 2006 in Hotel Atithi, Near Mumbai Airport Domestic Terminal. A plan of action in the couple of fields were discussed, which is in the process of finalization


NOVEMBER 18, 2006


We obtained our second registration under section 29 of Bombay Public Trusts Act 1950 vide Certificate No. F 31079 on 18 November 2006


OCTOBER 13, 2006


Mr. B. Bhattacharya, Secretary, Quality Forum delivered an invited talk on "Standards : Big benefits for small business" on 13 October 2006 in the Seminar on World Standards Day organized by the Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS) (WRO), Mumbai in MIDC, Andheri (E)


AUGUST 22, 2006


We organized and conducted our first open programme with the help and support of Quality Council of India (QCI) on Friday, 22 August 2006 in hotel Atithi, near domestic Terminal, Mumbai Airport. The topic of the Seminar was 'Quality Management Systems - requirements for service quality by public service organizations as per IS 15700:2005'.

At our request, Mr. Gidhar Gyani, the Secretary General of QCI made a special trip to Mumbai from Delhi and delivered an excellent inaugural address which was very informative for all. As against our original plan for 25 participants, there were 31 paid participants from various public sector organizations and consulting companies. Feedback from the participants gave a rating of 85.6% for Good & Excellent for the Seminar, which is quite encouraging.


FEBRUARY 3, 2006


We obtained our registration under section 7 of Society's Registration Act of 1860 vide Reg. No. 295/2006 on February 3, 2006
